Hello! Thank you for coming, let me introduce myself. I have been working as a Robotic Process Automation (RPA) Consultant for Propel Consult for two years now. As of July this year, I am the lead developer for our sister company, AutoMate, tailored for SMEs. We’re a small company of just 10 people, and earlier this year were awarded the number one partner in Europe by Automation Anywhere, the largest RPA vendor globally.

Outside of work I love weightlifting- I’m very proud of the fact I can hip thrust 3.5 times my body weight, but my favourite thing to do is go to the pub.

As a product of the extra time I’ve been afforded over the past year and a half, I’ve been trying out some different coding languages. After learning html and CSS from Harvard’s Intro to computer science course, I decided to make this blog. When I’ve looked for email chains and podcasts about relevant news in the field in the past I’ve always been underwhelmed by how mechanical and dull it all is. This blog is a less formal, chattier look at what’s been going on in the world of RPA. I hope you find my insights interesting - if so, I’d love to hear!
